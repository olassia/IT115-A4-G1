Thanksgiving from the perspective of a turkey

It seems these words are to be my last. I see my jailers crossing the muddy yard, their grim looks and lack of eye contact telling me everything I need to know. And in case I'm in any way unclear as to their intentions, one of them is carrying an axe...
So consider this my RSVP. It appears I'm available for dinner after all.
